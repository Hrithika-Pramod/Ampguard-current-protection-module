# Ampguard-current-protection-module
Introducing AmpGuard - Smart Protection for Sensitive Electronics

In today's world, devices like servo motors, LCDs, and LEDs play a crucial role in everyday systems. However, they are highly sensitive to issues like overcurrent and overheating, which can lead to failures and reduced lifespan.

a simple, To address this, we developed AmpGuard cost-effective protection and monitoring system designed specifically for low-voltage devices.

What AmpGuard does:

Monitors current and temperature in real-time

Detects abnormal conditions instantly

Protects devices by controlling power supply

Improves safety, reliability, and efficiency

Our research and survey analysis showed a strong need for such a solution, and the response to AmpGuard has been overwhelmingly positive.

While existing industrial solutions are often complex and expensive, AmpGuard focuses on accessibility, simplicity, and practical use.

This project reflects our effort to bridge the gap between advanced protection systems and everyday electronic applications.
